The story

Gold Coast kid. Winnetka high school. Now DePauw.

I spent my childhood in the Gold Coast, then moved up to Winnetka about five years ago, where I finished high school at North Shore Country Day. Now I’m a rising sophomore at DePauw University in Greencastle, Indiana, playing soccer for the Tigers. Go Tigers.

I’m also Social Chair of Phi Gamma Delta — which has taught me a lot about how to read a room, make people feel included, and keep the energy right. I even DJ a lot of our socials, which is honestly the same skill: read the room, keep everyone comfortable. That mindset carries into everything I do.

Outside of school and soccer, I volunteer with Connections for the Homeless in Evanston and spend time at local animal shelters. Looking out for people runs in my family.

The common thread: pay attention, be patient, and treat people like they matter. That’s how I approach Cutco. I work out of the Northbrook office under Danny Lewis — one of the best in this business — and reaching Key Salesman in 2026 means I know the products well enough to point you to exactly the right pieces, nothing extra. You’re not a lead to me. You’re someone I want to help make a smart decision and look after for years.

What’s next

Off to Spain this summer 🇪🇸

In a few weeks I head to Spain for 11 days with my DePauw soccer team — training and playing against sides out there, learning from their coaches, and soaking up the football culture at the source. I’m also brushing up on my español before we go (poco a poco 😅). Trips like this are part of why I do Cutco — it funds the experiences that make college unforgettable, and it keeps teaching me the same thing the field does: show up, work hard, and take care of the people around you.

The philosophy

“It’s not about the knife. It’s about the life you build with it.”

Most things are built to be replaced. Cutco is built to be kept — sharpened, used daily, and handed down. So I don’t try to sell you the most; I help you find the few pieces you’ll actually reach for every day, for the rest of your life.

Value over price
