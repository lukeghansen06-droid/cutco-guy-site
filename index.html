The Forever Guarantee, explained

It's the part people underestimate most. Here's what "guaranteed for life" actually means — in plain English.

✨

Forever sharp

When a blade loses its edge, send it in and Cutco sharpens it — free, for life. A small return shipping fee is all that applies.

🛠️

Performance, guaranteed

If your Cutco ever doesn't perform the way it should, Cutco corrects it or replaces the product. No drama.

👪

Travels with the owner

The guarantee follows the product, not the receipt — so gifts and hand-me-downs are covered the same as a first purchase.

↩️

15-day money back

New purchases come with a 15-day, no-questions money-back window. Try it in your kitchen first.

Even accidental damage is covered — Cutco will replace the item for half the current retail price, per their terms. Hard to beat.

How long does shipping take?

How long does it take?

About 20 minutes if you already know what you want, or a relaxed hour for the full experience. In person or over video — your call.

Is it really guaranteed for life?

Yes — Cutco's Forever Guarantee covers free sharpening and service for life, per Cutco's current terms (a small return-shipping fee applies). Ask me and I'll walk you through exactly how it works.

Honestly — why are some people skeptical of Cutco?

Fair question, and I'd rather answer it than dodge it. Cutco is sold by independent reps through Vector Marketing, and some people have run into pushy reps or heard "MLM" and tuned out. I get it. Here's my deal: it's not MLM — I don't recruit you to buy in, and I don't earn from a "downline." I just help you find the right cutlery, you buy directly from Cutco at the same prices listed on cutco.com, and the product is genuinely good and guaranteed for life. If I ever feel pushy, call me on it.

Are the reviews on here real?

100%. I'll only ever post words from actual people I've helped, with their permission — no stock quotes, no made-up names. Experiences vary person to person, but everything on the wall is genuine.
