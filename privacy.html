Privacy

This is a personal small-business site run by Luke Hansen. Here’s exactly what happens with your information — no legalese.

Analytics

This site uses Vercel Web Analytics, which collects anonymous, aggregate traffic data (page views, referrers, device type). It does not use cookies, does not track you across sites, and does not involve any ad networks or personal profiling.

Review form

When you submit a review, your name and review text are shown publicly on this site. Don’t include anything you wouldn’t want others to read.

Referral & “remind me” forms

If you enter contact details (a friend’s name, phone number, or email) in a referral or “remind me” form, that information is sent privately to Luke so he can follow up. It is never sold, shared with third parties, or published.

Booking

Demo scheduling is handled by Calendly. When you book, you’re using Calendly’s platform and their privacy policy applies. See calendly.com/privacy.

Remove your info

Want your review or contact details removed? Email lukeghansen06@gmail.com and Luke will take care of it promptly.
